# Teaching Notes

- One of three new topics started the same session, alongside [[../quantum-mechanics/NOTES.md|Quantum Mechanics]] and [[../algorithmic-info-theory/NOTES.md|Algorithmic Information Theory]].
- Strong Rust/C background — use programming-language analogies (types, generics, function composition) as the primary intuition pump, following the style of Bartosz Milewski's "Category Theory for Programmers."
- Comfortable with formal notation and proofs — move quickly through pure definitions, spend time on worked examples and diagrams instead.
