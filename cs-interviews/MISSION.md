# Mission: CS Interview Prep

## Why
To land a job at a modern, high-calibre company (Google-tier) by passing their technical interview process. The goal is not to memorise solutions but to build genuine fluency with the core patterns and concepts that underpin almost every interview problem.

## Success looks like
- Recognise which data structure or algorithm pattern a problem calls for within the first minute
- Implement clean solutions to medium-difficulty problems in Rust or C from scratch
- Explain trade-offs clearly: time complexity, space complexity, and why one approach beats another
- Comfortably discuss system design at a high level for distributed systems
- Pass a real technical screen at a company like Google, Meta, or a modern systems-focused company

## Constraints
- Preferred language: Rust (aspirationally — would love a Rust role). C as fallback.
- Targeting companies that are modern and technically strong
- Job change planned in the near future — urgency is real
- Learning through self-study with an AI teacher

## Out of scope
- Behavioural / HR interviews (separate prep)
- Deep system design (covered lightly — full deep-dive is a later topic)
- Language-specific trivia (syntax questions, not DS&A)
