# Mission: Emerging AI Research

## Why
To stay at the genuine frontier of AI research — understanding not just where AI is today but where it is heading, and why. JEPA, world models, next-generation architectures, and the ideas that may define the next decade of AI.

## Success looks like
- Understand Yann LeCun's JEPA vision and how it differs from current LLM paradigms
- Read a recent AI research paper (NeurIPS, ICML, arXiv) and extract the core idea
- Explain the limits of current LLMs and what architectures aim to address them
- Have an informed opinion on which research directions are most promising and why

## Constraints
- Strong software background; ml-frontier topic covers foundations
- This topic goes deeper into research — assumes transformer/attention basics

## Out of scope
- Replicating experiments (reading and understanding is the goal)
- AI policy and governance (separate interest if it emerges)
