# Mission: Finance & Markets

## Why
To understand how the financial world actually works — markets, the US economy, monetary policy, and how capital flows — so that I can make informed personal financial decisions and understand the forces that shape the world.

## Success looks like
- Read financial news and understand what it actually means
- Understand how the Fed's decisions ripple through markets and the economy
- Know how stocks, bonds, options, and other instruments work at a first-principles level
- Understand business cycles, recessions, and inflation
- Make confident, informed personal investment decisions

## Constraints
- No finance background assumed
- Practical understanding over academic theory

## Out of scope
- Day trading or active speculation
- Accounting and corporate finance internals (unless relevant)
