# Mission: Frontier Science

## Why
To understand the most awe-inspiring and counterintuitive parts of modern physics and science — quantum mechanics, relativity, and the emerging theories at the edge of human knowledge — at a level deeper than pop-science.

## Success looks like
- Explain quantum superposition, entanglement, and measurement without hand-waving
- Understand what quantum computing actually does (and doesn't) do
- Know the Standard Model at a conceptual level
- Understand why string theory and the multiverse are taken seriously by physicists
- Follow a physics paper at the abstract/introduction level

## Constraints
- No formal physics background beyond high school
- Mathematical rigour introduced gradually — intuition first
- Prefers "explain why this is weird" over textbook derivations

## Out of scope
- Engineering applications (quantum hardware specifics, etc.)
- Biology and chemistry (separate interests if they emerge)
