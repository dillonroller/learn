# Mission: Mindfulness

## Why
To develop a genuine, sustainable mindfulness practice — not as a productivity hack, but as a way of experiencing life more fully and responding to it less reactively.

## Success looks like
- Maintain a consistent daily practice (even just 10 minutes)
- Notice the difference between reacting and responding in real situations
- Understand the neuroscience behind why it works
- Know the roots of the practice and its relationship to Buddhist thought

## Constraints
- No prior meditation experience assumed
- Practical over ceremonial — secular framing preferred

## Out of scope
- Full Buddhist religious study (context only)
- Yoga or body-based practices (separate topic if desired)
