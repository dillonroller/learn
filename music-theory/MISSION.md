# Mission: Keyboard & Music Theory

## Why
To compose original music. Understanding how music works at a fundamental level — scales, harmony, rhythm, structure — and being able to express that at the keyboard, creating pieces that didn't exist before.

## Success looks like
- Improvise a melody over a chord progression without thinking about it
- Build a chord progression from scratch in any key
- Write and notate (or record) a short original piece with melody + harmony
- Understand why a piece of music sounds the way it does, not just how to play it

## Constraints
- Complete beginner: no prior instrument or theory experience
- Has a full keyboard (61+ keys)
- Learning through self-study with an AI teacher

## Out of scope
- Classical technique and formal performance (for now)
- Reading sheet music fluently (secondary to ear and composition)
- Music production / DAWs (may revisit once fundamentals are solid)
