# Baseline: Complete Beginner, Composition Goal

User is a complete beginner with no prior instrument or theory experience. They have a full keyboard (61+ keys) and their goal is to compose original music — not performance or classical technique. All theory should be framed through the lens of "how do you use this to write music?" Avoid performance-first framing.

**Implications:** Start from absolute zero (note names, keyboard layout). Skip notation fluency as a primary goal — prioritise ear, understanding chord/scale structure, and getting to a usable compositional vocabulary quickly.
