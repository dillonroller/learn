Topic overview

Rationality

Practical reasoning for real life — catching your own cognitive biases while they're happening, reasoning about uncertainty with actual probability instead of gut feeling, and disagreeing productively with people you're close to. Where Logic evaluates an argument's structure, this topic is about reasoning better in the moment, especially in relationships.
